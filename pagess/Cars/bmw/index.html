BMW, founded in 1916 by Karl Rapp, is known for its iconic luxury cars. The company's first car, the BMW Dixi, was introduced in 1927. In recent years, BMW has produced exceptional vehicles, with the latest innovations seen in the all-new BMW i4 electric car. The pinnacle of BMW luxury and performance is embodied by the BMW M8 Gran Coupe, the most expensive in the lineup. Among its popular models, the BMW 3 Series has stood as a global favorite for decades. In 2006, the Quandt family became the sole owners of BMW, ensuring its continued success and innovation in the automotive industry.
