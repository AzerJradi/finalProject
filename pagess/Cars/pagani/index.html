Pagani, the Italian supercar manufacturer, was founded in 1992 by Horacio Pagani. Their first car, the Zonda C12, was introduced in 1999. The latest model, the Pagani Huayra Roadster BC, boasts both performance and luxury. The Pagani Zonda HP Barchetta holds the title of their most expensive car. Among the most popular models is the Pagani Huayra, celebrated for its stunning design and power. In recent years, Horacio Pagani remains the owner and driving force behind the company, ensuring its continued success.
