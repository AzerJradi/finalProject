Ferrari, founded in 1947 by Enzo Ferrari, is an iconic Italian sports car manufacturer known for its legacy of performance and luxury. The company's first car, the 125 S, made its debut in 1947. In recent years, Ferrari's latest model, like the Ferrari SF90 Stradale, showcases cutting-edge technology and design. The LaFerrari Aperta stands as one of the most expensive cars in their lineup. Among the most popular models is the Ferrari 458 Italia. Currently, Ferrari is a part of the Stellantis group, with John Elkann serving as the Chairman.
