Audi, founded in 1909 by August Horch, has a storied history that began with the production of its first car, the Audi Type A. Over the years, Audi has evolved and now boasts an impressive lineup, with its latest models pushing the boundaries of automotive technology. The Audi R8 V10 Plus stands as the most expensive car in its range, while the Audi A3 is one of the most popular models worldwide. Today, Audi is a subsidiary of Volkswagen Group, having been acquired in 1966.
