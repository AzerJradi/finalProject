Bugatti, the iconic luxury car manufacturer, was founded in 1909 by Italian-born French automobile designer Ettore Bugatti. Their first production car was the Bugatti Type 13, introduced in 1910. The latest and one of the most expensive Bugatti cars is the Bugatti La Voiture Noire, unveiled in 2019 and priced at a staggering $18.68 million. The most popular Bugatti car is the Veyron, known for its exceptional speed and performance. Bugatti is now owned by the Volkswagen Group, a German automotive giant, which acquired the brand in 1998.
