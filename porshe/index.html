Porsche, a renowned German automotive manufacturer, was founded in 1931 by Ferdinand Porsche. The company's first car, the Porsche 64, was built in 1939. Porsche's latest models include various variants of the iconic 911 and the all-electric Taycan. The most expensive Porsche car currently available is the Porsche 911 GT2 RS, with a price exceeding $293,000. The most popular Porsche model is the Porsche 911, celebrated for its distinctive design and performance. As of my last knowledge update in January 2022, the Porsche company is majority-owned by Volkswagen Group.
